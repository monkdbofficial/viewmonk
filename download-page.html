🚀 Quick Start Guide

1

System Requirements

Ensure you have Node.js 18 or later installed on your system.

$ node --version
v18.0.0 or higher
2

Download & Extract

Download your preferred edition (LITE or FULL) and platform from above, then extract the archive to your desired location.

3

Launch Application

Linux/macOS:

$ ./start.sh

Windows:

Double-click start.bat
or run: node server.js
4

Access Interface

Open your browser and navigate to:

http://localhost:3000

For custom port configuration, use the PORT environment variable.
